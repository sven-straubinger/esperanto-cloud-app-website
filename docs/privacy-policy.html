Privacy Policy

Easy Translation - Shopify Translation App

Easy Translation – "the App” – provides management of translations – "the Service" – to merchants who use Shopify to power their stores.

This Privacy Policy describes how personal information is collected, used, and shared when you install or use the App in connection with your Shopify-supported store.

Information the App can access

When you install the App, we are automatically able to access certain types of information from your Shopify account:

  • Translations and Locales
  • Legal Policies
  • Content, e.g. pages or articles
  • Templates for email and SMS notifications
  • Products, variants and collections

The access is needed to provide translation services when using the app.

We do not persist any of those data described above.
We do not access customer-related data.

Information the App collects

When you install the App, we are technically required to persist certain types of information from your Shopify account in order to provide the Service:

  • Shopify account data (Shop URL, API token, install state)

When you remove the app, we erase your shop-related data. Shopify will notify us by sending a request 48 hours after removal.

How Do We Use Your Personal Information?

We use the personal information we collect from you in order to provide the Service and to operate the App. Additionally, we use this personal information to: Communicate with you; Optimize or improve the App;

You won't receive Spam from us.

Sharing Your Personal Information

Finally, we may also share your Personal Information to comply with applicable laws and regulations, to respond to a subpoena, search warrant or other lawful request for information we receive, or to otherwise protect our rights.

Infrastructure

Firebase

We use Firebase as third party subprocessor to provide the technical infrastructure and the Service of the App. Firebase is a platform developed by Google LLC (1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, California, U.S.) for creating modern web applications. It is used for hosting, data storage and communication via HTTPS. The App acts as a broker between your store and Shopify services. In general, Firebase monitors info- & error-events occurring in the App and/or backend services, and collects data including your IP address, browser type, Internet service provider, and date/time stamps. Following Firebase Services are used by us to provide the Service:

  • Cloud Firestore
  • Cloud Functions for Firebase
Please see the Terms of Service for Firebase Services for more information on how data is processed.

Compliance with the GDPR is a top priority for Google. Google takes a customer-centric approach on protection, control, and compliance:

  • Privacy and Security in Firebase
  • Firebase Data Processing and Security Terms

DeepL

We use DeepL API as third party subprocessor to provide automated translations for a subset of languages. DeepL is a platform developed by DeepL SE (Maarweg 165, 50825 Köln, Germany) for creating machine translations. Texts or documents you submit will not be permanently stored and will only be kept temporarily to the extent necessary for the production and transmission of the translation. After complete performance of the contractually agreed services all submitted texts or documents and their translations will be deleted. Your texts will not be used to improve the quality of the DeepL services.

For further information on the processing of your data within DeepL (DeepL Pro subscription), please refer to section 5 of the DeepL privacy policy and the DeepL Pro Terms and Conditions.
